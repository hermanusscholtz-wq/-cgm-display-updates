Leah CGM Display Firmware v0.10.12

Changes:
- Cleans up the 4-hour trend screen top row.
- Removes current glucose value from the trend top row so Wi-Fi bars do not overlap text.
- Keeps compact 4H and IN% labels only on the top row.
- Keeps dynamic Y-axis scaling so high glucose values such as 18 mmol/L remain visible on the trend.
- Keeps enlarged full-window trend graph from v0.10.11.
- Keeps LOW/HIGH alarm-limit lines and time-in-range calculations.
- Keeps night dimming, direct web screen links, update envelope, diagnostics, local setup, security direction, and OTA direction.

Important:
The trend Y-axis dynamically scales to the data received from Nightscout and the active alarm limits.
The time-in-range value is calculated locally from the Nightscout entries received by the device.
This display is a secondary CGM awareness and alert companion.
It is not intended to replace the primary CGM app, receiver, or approved medical device.
