Leah CGM Display Firmware v0.11.0

Changes:
- Adds optional UART external audio / Bluetooth module framework.
- Designed for a DFR0782-style UART-controlled audio module.
- Adds Audio / Bluetooth OLED screen.
- Repurposes GPIO27 as AUDIO / MUTE button:
  - Short press during alarm: mute/snooze audio/alarm.
  - Short press without alarm: enable/disable optional audio module and show Audio screen.
  - Long press: enable/disable optional audio module and show Audio screen.
- Adds local webserver Audio / Bluetooth Alarm Module settings:
  - Enable/disable audio module.
  - UART baud rate.
  - Normal and urgent volume.
  - Voice track numbers for LOW, URGENT LOW, HIGH, URGENT HIGH, and NO DATA.
  - Repeat interval minutes for each alarm type.
- Adds webserver test buttons:
  - Test LOW voice.
  - Test URGENT LOW voice.
  - Test HIGH voice.
  - Test URGENT HIGH voice.
  - Test NO DATA voice.
  - Stop audio.
- Adds non-blocking alarm voice repeat scheduler:
  - LOW default every 5 min.
  - URGENT LOW default every 2 min.
  - HIGH default every 15 min.
  - URGENT HIGH default every 5 min.
  - NO DATA default every 10 min.
- Keeps visual alarms active even when audio is disabled or unavailable.
- Keeps v0.10.12 trend, OTA, webserver, diagnostics, dimming, and setup features.

Bench-test note:
This build sends generic UART text commands:
  VOL:<volume>
  PLAY:<track>
  STOP
When the DFR0782 arrives, confirm the module's exact UART command protocol and update sendAudioSetVolume(), sendAudioPlayTrack(), and sendAudioStop() if required.

Important:
This display is a secondary CGM awareness and alert companion.
It is not intended to replace the primary CGM app, receiver, or approved medical device.
Voice prompts must be treated as supportive guidance only.
